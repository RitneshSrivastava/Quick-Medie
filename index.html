About Quick Medie

Founder Ritnesh and Co-founder Dee from Chandigarh University, Quick Medie was born to solve a big pain: no one was delivering first aid or checking fever at home when people needed it most. So we built India’s first doorstep first aid platform—bringing you care for wounds, viral fever, allergies, BP, and more. Quick Medie is on a mission to bridge the gap between panic and care. With Quick Medie, I aim to bring timely, doorstep-first aid and urgent care services to people when they need it the most — no queues, no confusion, just care. Because in India, not every emergency is a 108 situation — but every cry for help deserves an answer. "Jab tak hospital door hai, tab tak Quick Medie aapke saath hai."
